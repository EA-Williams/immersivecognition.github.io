BIGKAT

The Boxed infrared Gross Kinematic Assessment Tool (BIGKAT) is an optical motion capture system, which captures the movement of infrared emitting diodes (IRED) in 3-dimensional space.
Born in Bradford

Born in Bradford is one of the largest research studies in the World, tracking the lives of over 30,000 Bradfordians to find out what influences the health and wellbeing of families.
UXF: Unity Experiment Framework

An open source package for the Unity3D game engine that can be used to assist the development of virtual reality human behaviour experiments.
